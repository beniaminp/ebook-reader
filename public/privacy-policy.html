Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

EBook Reader ("the App") is an open-source ebook reading application. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your choices.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data is stored locally on your device.

  • Books and files you import are stored on-device only (in the app's local database and IndexedDB storage).
  • Reading progress, bookmarks, highlights, and settings are saved locally and never sent to external servers.
  • Reading statistics are computed and stored on your device.

Network Access

The App may access the internet in the following optional scenarios:

  • Metadata enrichment: When you import a book, the App may query public book metadata APIs (e.g., Google Books, Open Library) to fetch cover images and descriptions. Only the book title and author are sent; no personal data is transmitted.
  • OPDS catalog browsing: If you choose to connect to an OPDS feed or Calibre-Web server, the App communicates with the server URL you provide.
  • P2P sharing (web only): If you opt in to share a book via WebTorrent, the file is shared peer-to-peer. This feature is not available on Android.

None of these features transmit personal information, account data, or device identifiers.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no ads in the App.

Permissions

On Android, the App may request the following permissions:

  • Storage / file access: To import ebook files from your device.
  • Internet: For the optional network features described above.

Data Retention

All app data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the App removes all associated data. The App does not maintain any server-side data storage.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from children. Since no personal data is collected from any user, the App is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.

EBook Reader is open-source software. This privacy policy applies to the official builds distributed via GitHub and Google Play.
